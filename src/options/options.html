Calculate the time it takes to earn what you spend
How it works
  • This extension shows you how many days it would take you to earn the amount that you are spending online.
  • Based on the product price amount, it calculates the time it would take you to pay for the product by dividing the price amount by 30 (days in a month).
  • On supported online shopping websites, along with each product price, this extension will show the number of days calculated and highlight the price according to the colour settings set below.
  • It is assumed that the currency in which you shop is the same currency in which you earn, and hence, you only need to enter the salary amount without currency

List of supported websites

  • Amazon
  • Flipkart
  • Ajio
  • Myntra
  • eBay
  • OLX
  • Meesho
  • Takealot
  • Your site not listed? Let me know here
